Solving the Rubik's Cube 🧊

Cube literacy — reading the cube and understanding why each move works, not chanting algorithms.

The goal: pick up any scrambled cube, read its state in terms of pieces (not stickers), solve it start to finish unaided, and explain what each stage accomplishes and why.
